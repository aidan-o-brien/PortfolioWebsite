About

Hi, my name is Aidan O'Brien and I love working with data, eating great food and playing various sports.

After spending a year in finance at Novia Financial Plc and finishing my undergraduate in Economics, I decided to take my interest in data and statistics further. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I completed a Master's degree in Data Science where I learnt about the power of Python, SQL and machine learning.

Now, I enjoy using my skills in data cleaning, modelling and visualisation to derive new insights from data, as well as produce useful tools for people - check out my portfolio!

I am on the hunt for an interesting and challenging role as a Data Scientist or a Data Analyst. Although I am currently based in Manchester, I am open to working in London and other areas.

Researchers often have to scour the scientific literature to understand complex topics. Tools like Connected Papers help with this process by visualising parts of the citation network to higlight influential papers. However, network diagrams can become complex and easily misinterpreted. Science and Start-ups

Start-ups and science have had a turbulent history together. On one hand you have BioNTech, a start-up founded in 2008 that laid the foundations for the first COVID-19 vaccine with their innovative mRNA technology. On the other hand, you have Theranos, a fraudulent blood-testing start-up that warned us of the dangers of science-based start-ups.
To investigate the relationship between start-ups and science, this project created a novel data set, conducted statistical testing and used natural language processing techniques.

Soft skills: Project Management | Report Writing
Hard skills: Data Collection | Data Cleaning | Data Visualisation | NLP | Hypothesis Generation | Statistical Testing
Tools: Python | Tableau | Gensim | Pandas | Stata

Traffic Counter

A web application that allows users to count traffic at different locations. Users can log-in using their username and password and then proceed to add records of traffic that they observe. The user could view their session summary before logging out. Additional Python scripts query the SQL database to provide summary statistics and automate data entry.

Hard skills: Data collection | Data cleaning
